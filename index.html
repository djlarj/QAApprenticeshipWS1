Sunny Days at Sesame Street?

Join us to learn what it is really like to live on Sesame Street.

The Speakers

Hear their stories first hand from our beloved residents themselves. Learn about their struggles, hardships, and how they manage to survive this long. Sesame Street is not the happy place you grew up thinking it was. This will be an emotional night you will never forget!

Oscar the Grouch

Oscar the Grouch is a furry, green Grouch who lives in a trash can on Sesame Street. In fact, he loves trash so much that he's rarely seen outside of his trash can. His trademark song, explaining his passion for refuse, is "I Love Trash."

@oscarthegrouch
Mr. Snuffleupagus

Mr. Snuffleupagus is a full-bodied Muppet character who lives with his family in a cave just off of Sesame Street. His species is Snuffleupagus as well as his last name.

@mrsnuffleupagus
Prairie Dawn

Prairie Dawn is a level-headed, now 18-year-old Muppet young lady on Sesame Street. She was known for writing and directing school pageants featuring her friends, mostly Ernie, Bert, Herry, Grover, and Cookie Monster.

@prairiedawn
The Count

Count von Count is a mysterious but friendly vampire-like Muppet on Sesame Street who is meant to parody Bela Lugosi's portrayal of Count Dracula. He first appeared on Sesame in 1972, counting blocks with Bert and Ernie.

@thecount
Mystery Guest

This event will be hosting a special Sesame Street Mystery Guest. All we can say is that the Mystery Guest is a Sesame Street character and the only way to find out who the Mystery Guest will be is to attend the event.

@mysteryguest
Susan Robinson

Susan Robinson is a psychiatrist who has her practice on Sesame Street. She was a former public health nurse when she first arrived at Sesame Street. She is married to Gordon Robinson, a high school science teacher.

@susanrobinson
